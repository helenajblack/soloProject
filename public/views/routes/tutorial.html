Tutorial

Welcome to urgenC.


urgenC is an app designed to let users access emergency services during times they are unable to speak - whether that be because of disability, or if you find yourself in a situation where you must be silent.

Symbols

Tap to return to this tutorial page.

The Prompt System

This tutorial will lead you through a hypothetical situation.

Upon tapping the emergency button, you will enter the prompt system. Immediately you will see the words

Please state your emergency

You will given several options. Select the one that best describes your situation, such as:

Upon making a selection, more questions will be loaded. These questions are designed to gather the most information possbile from you about your emergency. These are all qustions that would be asked by 911 dispatch.

It is highly recommended that you answer all possible questions, but if at any point you are unable to continue answering, hit:

You can find the Call Now button at the bottom of the Prompt page - simply scroll down until you see it.

Once you hit Call Now the answers you gave to the prompt questions will be converted into an audio file. You'll be asked to share your location - your phone's GPS will locate you, and then provide that location as well as the audio file to 911 dispatch.

At this point, it is imperative that you leave your phone on. If you are concerned about the noise, feel free to turn down your volume. 911 dispatch will be informed that you are using the urgenC app and that you cannot speak.

Thank you for using urgenC!
